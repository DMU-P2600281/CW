Chadwick Boseman

Black Panther

Chadwick Boseman was an American actor. He was born on the 29th November 1976 and unfortunately passed away on 28th August 2020. He starred in 15 films and 16 television programmes. His best film in my opinion,by far, is Black Panther but the film where he had his big break was as a baseball player Jackie Robinson in the film '42'. He was nominated for 37 awards and won 10 of them,this man achieved so much in his life that he lived,may he rest in eternal peace.

Letitia Wright

Shuri

Letitia Wright plays the sister of Black Panther (Shuri) and has a range of films and television shows which she has starred in. She is 27 years of age from the UK where she moved from Georgetown in Guyana when she was seven years old and grew up in Tottenham. She has won four awards,being: Teen Choice Awards for Choice Sci-Fi movie actress,British Academy Film Awards for Rising Star Award,NAACP Image Awards for Outstanding Breakthrough Performance in a Motion Picture and Screen Actors Guild Awards for Outstanding Performance by a Cast in a Motion Picture.

Michael B. Jordan

Killmonger

Michael B Jordan played Erik "Killmonger" Stevens in Black Panther.He is 33 years old and was born 9th February 1987,he was born in California but moved to New Jersey where he attended Newark Arts High School and played basketball. Michael started his career in 1999 as a model for various companies then moved onto his career as an actor. He first began to appear in shows very briefly such as Cosby and The Sopranos.
